about julie

Graduating from the University of Texas at Austin with a Bachelor of Science in Advertising, Julie has a passion and insight for understanding what clients and users want. She is a self-taught programmer and first learned Full-Stack Web Development building Ruby on Rails web applications. Her current favorite programming language of choice is JavaScript, but she is always open and eager to learn and master new things whether it's a new programming language, framework or design skills.


She currently works as a Front-End Developer at the City of Bellevue but also runs her own freelance design + creative studio building websites for businesses and entrepreneurs.
