about julie

Graduating from the University of Texas at Austin with a B.S. in Advertising, With her background in Marketing & Advertising, Julie has a passion and insight for understanding what clients and users want. She is a self-taught programmer and first learned Full-Stack Web Development creating Ruby on Rails web applications. Her current favorite programming language of choice is JavaScript, but she is always open and eager to learn and master new things whether it's a new programming language, frameworks or design skills.

She is currently a Front-End Developer at the City of Bellevue (in the Seattle Area) but also runs her own freelance design + creative studio building websites for businesses and entrepreneurs.

Aside from coding & designing, Julie also loves to travel, hike, take pictures, and overall explore new places and find the best coffeeshops and food spots. She is currently based out of Seattle, WA with her husband Brent and their lovable cute dog named Harvey. (follow him on Instagram)
